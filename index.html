About me

I'm currently a student and a small business owner. Alongside my academics, I have always been drawn to art and aesthetics as a way of expressing myself. What began as a quiet interest gradually turned into something more meaningful over time. I chose to build this brand to give that passion a purpose beyond just a hobby. The idea is to create pieces that feel personal, thoughtful and lasting. This journey began during the Covid period, when I found myself with time to explore new interests. I started experimenting with painting, which slowly grew into an interest in crafts and DIY creations. What started as a way to pass time has now become a space where I create and share.
